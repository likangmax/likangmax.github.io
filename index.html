Biography

I am a PhD student in Probability and Mathematical Statistics at Beijing Normal-Hong Kong Baptist University, supervised by Prof. Jianxin Pan. I received my Master's degree from Hong Kong Baptist University and Bachelor's degree from Henan University.

My research focuses on computational statistics and machine learning:

  • Scalable algorithms for high-dimensional statistical computing
  • Statistical methods for large language models
  • Applications in healthcare analytics and biomedical informatics
