Bio

I am a PhD student in Probability and Mathematical Statistics at BNU-HKBU United International College, supervised by Prof. Jianxin Pan. Prior to joining UIC, I received my Master's degree from Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) and Bachelor's degree from Henan University.

My research focuses on developing efficient computational methods for complex statistical models, with particular emphasis on:

  • High-performance algorithms for large-scale statistical computing
  • Optimization methods in statistical inference
  • Statistical software development (R packages)
  • Applications in medical data analysis and bioinformatics
